The Story of ZX0

ZX0, the brilliant mind behind Plasma, started this journey back in early 2023. Frustrated by slow and key-locked executors, he dreamt of creating a fast, keyless, and beautiful solution. After months of learning, testing, and building from scratch, Plasma was born — sleek, powerful, and completely free.

ZX0 spent countless nights debugging crashes, optimizing injection speeds, and crafting the ultimate experience for every Roblox scripter. Plasma V4 is the result of that dedication — an executor that feels like magic and performs like a beast.
